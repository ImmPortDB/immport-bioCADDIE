#############################
Mapping ImmPort to bioCADDIE
#############################
This is an overview of how the ImmPort schema was mapped to the
bioCADDIE DATS Specification v2.1.

.. note:: ImmPort is funded by the NIH, NIAID and DAIT in support of the NIH
          mission to share data with the public. Data shared through ImmPort
          has been provided by NIH-funded programs, other research
          organizations and individual scientists ensuring these discoveries
          will be the foundation of future research.
